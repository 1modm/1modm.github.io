About

Hello there, thank you so much for visiting my blog!

I have presented myself online with a pseudonym known as “Heiswayi Nrird” since I met the Internet. In the real world, people have been calling me with the short name “Wayi”. I currently have been working in Keysight Technologies as a R&D Software Engineer in Penang, Malaysia. A more detailed look at my professional skills and experiences can be found on LinkedIn or you may have a look at my online resume here.

This website (weblog/blog) is a place to share my thoughts, ideas, projects and findings. It’s built with the excellent Jekyll and proudly hosted on GitHub. Previously, I have a self-hosted WordPress blog at my domain (wayi.me/blog), but since I’m not a rigid blogger, I made a move to use Jekyll for some reasons.
